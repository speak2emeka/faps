Unified education

A smooth journey from first steps to confident leadership

FAPS nurtures early years and primary learners, while Royal Prestige Leadership Academy builds secondary students through rigorous academics, character formation, digital fluency, and leadership practice.

  • First Age Private School: Pre-Nursery, Nursery, and Primary education with caring foundational support.
  • Royal Prestige Leadership Academy: Junior and Senior Secondary education for academic depth and leadership growth.
  • Future-ready learning: Coding, robotics kits, science labs, engineering challenges, and competitions.
