Early Years

Pre-Nursery and Nursery learners build language, social confidence, early numeracy, creativity, and routines through guided play.

Primary School

Primary learners develop literacy, numeracy, science thinking, digital habits, arts, values, and problem-solving skills.

Junior Secondary

JSS students deepen core subjects, practical science, computing, leadership, clubs, and independent study habits.

Senior Secondary

SSS students prepare for external examinations, career pathways, leadership roles, and advanced STEM exposure.

Curriculum

Balanced, measurable, and practical

The curriculum supports national requirements while adding structured digital literacy, project work, public speaking, leadership practice, and STEM experiences.

  • Teaching philosophy: clear instruction, guided practice, collaboration, and reflection.
  • Assessment: classwork, projects, quizzes, term examinations, practical demonstrations, and parent feedback.
  • Support: differentiated tasks, mentoring, enrichment, and intervention where needed.
STEM and Robotics

Coding, robotics kits, engineering challenges, labs, and competitions

Learners build practical skills through coding, robotics, science investigation, teamwork, and innovation projects.
