Can a child move from FAPS into Royal Prestige?

Yes. The school structure is designed for a smooth transition from primary into junior secondary.

Are STEM and Robotics available to all learners?

Activities are introduced progressively by age and level, from digital literacy to robotics and competitions.

How do I confirm current fees?

Contact admissions for the current approved fee schedule and payment guidance.
