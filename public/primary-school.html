FAPS Primary Admin

Dedicated support for younger learners and families

The Primary School admin handles nursery and primary enquiries, class placement, parent communication, early-years routines, health notes, class reminders, and age-appropriate activities.

  • Admin Office: First Age Private School Administration.
  • Levels: Pre-Nursery, Nursery, and Primary.
  • Communication: parent notices, class updates, early-years routines, fees guidance, and visit scheduling.
  • Focus: care, literacy, numeracy, confidence, play, curiosity, and good habits.

Primary Admin Desk

Handles: FAPS admissions, class allocation, parent enquiries, nursery/primary newsletters, excursions, early-years events, and pupil records.

Best for: parents of Pre-Nursery, Nursery, and Primary pupils.
