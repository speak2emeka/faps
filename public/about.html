History

From strong foundations to leadership preparation

The school was established to give children a caring start, a rigorous primary experience, and a confident secondary pathway. FAPS serves Pre-Nursery, Nursery, and Primary learners. Royal Prestige Leadership Academy extends the journey through Junior and Senior Secondary.

  • Safe, structured early learning.
  • Primary education that develops literacy, numeracy, curiosity, and confidence.
  • Secondary education focused on examination success, leadership, and digital fluency.
